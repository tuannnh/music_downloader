🎵 Download to Copytele

Paste a YouTube or TikTok video URL. Best-quality audio is extracted and uploaded to copytele.

On iPhone, tap “Add iPhone Shortcut”, then open the downloaded file in the Shortcuts app. Requires Settings → Shortcuts → Allow Untrusted Shortcuts (one-time). After that, share any YouTube/TikTok video → Save Music to Copytele.
